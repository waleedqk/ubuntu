# i3 Tiles

**Open a window**

    Mod+Enter

**Tile window to the right**

    Mod+Enter

**Tile window Vertically**

    Mod+v
    Mod+Enter

**Move among tiles**

    Mod+<arrow-key>
    Mod+<j,k,l,;>

**Stack the windows**

    Mod+s

**Move among stack**

    Mod+<arrow-key>

**From stack to tile**

    Mod+e

**Tab the windows**
    
    Mod+w

**Move among tabs**

    Mod+<arrow-key>

**From tab to tile**

    Mod+e
